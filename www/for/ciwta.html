Tootsville is operated by the Corporation for Inter-World Tourism and Adventuring.

Tootsville's fictional island of Tootanga is a world where players can build, learn, and play together. The Corporation for Inter-World Tourism and Adventuring is the nonprofit organization who operates Tootsville.

Tootsville is free for everyone and the efforts of the Corporation for Inter-World Tourism and Adventuring help ensure that it remains a fun, safe place for everyone.

You can learn more about the Corporation for Inter-World Tourism and Adventuring at our web site, ciwta.org.
