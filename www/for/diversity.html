Tootsville is for Diversity!

In Tootsville, players control colorful, magical Toots characters. Using the magic of wishes, Toots can be any color they want, wear special outfits, and explore different appearances.

Learning about others is easier when you can walk a mile in their shoes &emdash; and through the power of wishing magic (and computers!) you can explore different lives.

Tootsville is a fun virtual world where anybody can play, build, and explore for free!
